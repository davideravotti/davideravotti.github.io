ChaParDyn Reading Group



Meetings usually take place at 10am on Wednesdays in the second floor seminar room in Howard House.
A list of the previous meetings can be found at the 2015-16 Reading Group Webpage.

We are using the following material:

  • [1]   Ratner's Theorem on SL(2,R)-invariant measures, M. Einsiedler, Jahresber. Deutsch. Math.-Verein., 108(3), 143–164, 2006. (Preprint available here).
  • [2]   The cohomological equation for Roth-type interval exchange maps, S. Marmi, P. Moussa and J.-C. Yoccoz, Journal of the AMS, 18(4), 823-872, 2005.
  • [3]   Affine interval exchange maps with a wandering interval, S. Marmi, P. Moussa and J.-C. Yoccoz, Proc. London Math. Soc., 100(3), 639-669, 2009.
  • [4]   Ratner's Theorems on unipotent flows, D.W. Morris, Chicago Lectures in Mathematics Series, 2005.
  • [5]   Ergodic infinite group extensions of geodesic flows on translation surfaces, D. Ralston and S.E. Troubetzkoy, Journal of Modern Dynamics, 6, 477-497, 2012. (Preprint available here).
