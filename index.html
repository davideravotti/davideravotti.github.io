I have completed my Ph.D. at the University of Bristol in 2018 under the supervision of Corinna Ulcigrai.

From April to August 2018, I was a Research Associate at the University of Bristol, and from September 2018 to February 2019 I was a Postdoctoral Assistant at the University of Zürich.

Since March 2019, I am a Research Fellow at Monash University, working with Andy Hammerlindl.

Here you can find my full CV.

I study the ergodic and mixing properties of some classes of smooth dynamical systems.

I am interested in smooth parabolic flows, in particular locally Hamiltonian flows on compact surfaces, time-changes of nilflows, and perturbations of unipotent flows on quotients of semisimple Lie groups, and, more recently, in partially hyperbolic diffeomorphisms.
